## High concept ideas

### 1. Climbing game
 - FPP version of PEAK? ICEPEAK?
 - Resource management like stamina, hunger
 - Choose the best path to get to top
 - Utilize VR to grab items and see items on body
 - Hazards like falling rocks and breaking ice
 - Climbing ice bergs / iced waterfalls
 - Have unbreakable platforms for climbing
 - All other iced surfaces break after 2 seconds of inserting the ice pick


### 2. Coop / competition involving both Bird's eye and Worm's eye views
 - Theme: bank heist getaway — one player is the robber on the ground, the other is a partner piloting a drone overhead
 - Robber (worm's eye, first-person): runs through city streets, alleys, and buildings to escape pursuing cops
 - Drone pilot (bird's eye, top-down): sees the full city map, spots cop positions and patrols, and calls out safe routes
 - Drone pilot can interact with the environment: hack traffic lights, lock doors behind the robber, create distractions
 - Robber can pick up disguises, use shortcuts only visible at ground level, and hide in crowds
 - Cops have dynamic AI patrols that adapt to the robber's last known position
 - Win by reaching the getaway vehicle before time runs out or getting caught

### 3. Slice and dice
 - VR sword / blade combat where the player slices through waves of incoming objects or enemies
 - Objects must be cut along specific highlighted lines or angles for bonus points
 - Different blade types (katana, dual daggers, cleaver) with unique swing speeds and slice widths
 - Physics-based slicing: cut pieces react realistically, splitting and falling apart
 - Combo system rewarding rhythm and accuracy of consecutive slices
 - Environmental hazards mixed in (bombs, shields) that the player must dodge or deflect instead of slice
